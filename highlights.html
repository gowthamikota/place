Highlights

Adiyogi Statue

The largest bust sculpture in the world, located in Coimbatore, India. The Adiyogi statue stands as a symbol of spiritual evolution and the potential for inner transformation.

Inner Engineering Programs

Transformative practices for physical and mental well-being. These programs are designed to help individuals discover their inner potential through powerful tools for personal growth.

Social Initiatives

The foundation runs several social initiatives, such as Rally for Rivers, Project GreenHands, and more, aiming to bring about social and environmental change across India and the world.
